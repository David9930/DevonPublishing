A Letter Guide For Rebecca

What if you could have all of your current memories and experiences transferred into your twelve-year-old self...

Get Your Copy

The Intriguing Premise

Would you try to prevent 9/11 from happening?

With foreknowledge of one of history's greatest tragedies, what choices would you make?

Or save Princess Di?

Could you alter the fate of one of the world's most beloved figures?

Or how about whacking some Narcissistic President?

Would you interfere with the course of political history?

Or maybe bet on a few sporting events?

How would you use your knowledge for personal gain?

Or even better, memorize a few lottery numbers!

Would wealth be your priority with this second chance?

The Protagonist's Journey

Derek Devon is an aging 63-year-old, divorced, single and about to retire. He never would have imagined that a simple trip into his buddy's fly-in fish camp would result in a life-changing and out-of-this-world opportunity that would allow him to re-do much of his lifetime regrets and most of all, try to win the heart of his High School Dream girl that he lost so many years ago.

"A Letter Guide For Rebecca" will transport you back to when you were twelve and you will be able to unlock many of your own memories. Some good, and some not so good.

A Unique Individual

Derek was always a unique individual and his decisions on how to live a redo will take you on some exciting highs and some lows.

The Superpower

Having the Superpower of "Knowing what would happen", will allow Derek to not only go back for his do-over but use his ability to try and make the world a better place.

Consequences

Remember, "Changes have consequences and consequences make change". You will learn his choices and how he planned to execute those choices. Would you do the same or would you do things differently?

Cosmic Implications

If you think we are alone in the Universe, THINK AGAIN! And what happens when a simple emotional desire to try and win the heart of your high school sweetheart infects our alien visitors? And not in a good way...

Finally, the story is in fact a love story. My love story, and it is not yet finished... While my journey continues to find a new 'It-Girl', my current 'It-Girl' remains firmly etched in my memories.... Now, never to be forgotten!
